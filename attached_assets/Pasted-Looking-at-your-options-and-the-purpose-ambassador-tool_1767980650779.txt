Looking at your options and the purpose (ambassador tool for customer conversations), here's my analysis and suggested naming structure:

## **Recommended Names:**

### **Primary Tool Name:**
**"Security Exposure Check"** or **"Data Breach Checker"**

### **For Ambassador Use:**
**"Trust Builder Scanner"** or **"Security Conversation Starter"**

---

## **Why These Work Better:**

**"Security Exposure Check"** is:
- Clear and professional
- Sounds like a medical check-up (familiar analogy)
- Non-technical customers understand "exposure"
- Action-oriented ("check" implies something to do)

**"Data Breach Checker"** is:
- Direct and specific
- Uses common terminology ("data breach" is widely understood)
- Creates urgency without being alarmist
