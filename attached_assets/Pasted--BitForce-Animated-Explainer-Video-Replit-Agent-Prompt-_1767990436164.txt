- Color Palette: Use BitForce brand colors (or professional tech palette if brand colors unknown)

- Voiceover: "That's where BitForce comes in - your digital empowerment partner."

**SCENE 3: OUR SOLUTIONS (20-40 seconds)**
**Visual Sequence A: Security**
- Shield icon protecting data streams
- Lock symbols securing devices
- Text: "Advanced Digital Security"

**Visual Sequence B: AI Integration**
- AI characters assisting humans
- Neural networks connecting
- Text: "Smart AI Companions"

**Visual Sequence C: Empowerment**
- People gaining digital superpowers
- Ambassadors growing network
- Text: "Ambassador Opportunity"

**SCENE 4: THE OPPORTUNITY (40-50 seconds)**
- Visual: Network of connected nodes growing, ambassadors rising up
- Text: "Join the BitForce Movement"

**SCENE 5: CALL TO ACTION (50-60 seconds)**

**ANIMATION STYLE GUIDE:**

**Visual Elements:**
1. **Characters:** Simplified humanoid figures (gender-neutral, diverse)
2. **Icons:** Clean line icons with smooth animations
3. **Transitions:** Smooth wipes, fades, and morphing effects
4. **Data Visualization:** Flowing data streams, particle effects, network grids
5. **Text Animation:** Clean typewriter or fade-in effects

**Motion Principles:**
- Smooth easing on all movements
- Consistent animation speed throughout
- Purposeful transitions between concepts
- Highlight key elements with subtle scale/pulse animations

**TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:**

**Tools to Use (Replit-Compatible):**
1. **SVG Animation:** Create vector graphics and animate with CSS/JavaScript
2. **Canvas Animation:** Use HTML5 Canvas for complex particle effects
3. **Video Export:** Use FFmpeg or similar to render final MP4
4. **Audio:** Add royalty-free background music and optional voiceover
